Mini-Muse is a research project developed by Institute of Design SUPSI and Dalle Molle Institute for Artificial Intelligence USI-SUPSI in partnership with ETH-Zurich Library.

The project runs from October 2023 to September 2024 and is funded by Hasler Foundation.

Research team: Dr. Giovanni Profeta (principal investigator), Dr. Fabio Rinaldi, Joseph Cornelius.

ETH-Zurich Library team: Regina Wanger (reference contact), Michael Gasser, Sibille Christiane, Ehrismann Michael.
